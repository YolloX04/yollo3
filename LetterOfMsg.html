                "The Bird and The Tree"

If you were a hibernating bird,
I'd be the tree you find refuge in.
In the chill of winter's grasp,
I'd kindle a campfire to warm your soul within.

When icy winds from the north do blow,
I'd build a fortress to shelter you from the cold.
I aspire to be a steadfast wall,
Guarding your mind and body, as time unfolds.

                "The Perfect Harmony"

If you were a writer,
I would be the book you write.
If you were a cook,
I would be the dish you made.
If you were a melody,
I would be the harmony.

My life was black and white,
And you would be the artist that paint my life.

                "Little May"

Little May, My heart is true
Stick with you like a glue.
Little May, I won't lie to you
Falling into you like a fool.

Little May, be my other half
I humbly seek your heart.
Little May, express my love with this poem
Sincerely craving your heart as its eternal home.

                "Guardian of Your Heart"

Wherever you go, I will be your guide.
Whenever you feel lost, I will be your light
Whenever you face adversity, I will be by your side.
Don't worry, I will protect you with all my might.

Whatever you do, I will support.
Whoever hurt you, I will block.
Whenever you are glad, I will rock.
Don't worry, Keeping you safe is my job.

                "Love Everything of You"

The way you smile makes my heart fly.
The way you speak makes my ear sweet.
The way you touch makes my face blush.
The way you love makes me feel loved.

The way you dream makes my inspiration.
The way you hug makes my breathe rush.
I love everything of you.
It just needs to be you.

                "The Nature of Us"

The sun rises from East to West.
The river flows from North to South.
The compass shows only North.
Like the nature,
My heart directs only towards you.
My soul attach only with you.
My whole life give to you.
I call this, my nature
May, you will be my future.
